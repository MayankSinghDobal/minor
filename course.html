Our Programs

We offer a wide variety of programs such as BJJ, Muay Thai, and MMA that are designed to keep you active, build confidence, stay focused, and get fit as you learn to protect yourself.

Brazilian Jiu Jitsu (BJJ) Classes in Union

Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu enhances physical fitness, agility, and mental discipline. Practitioners develop effective ground-based combat skills, resilience, and adaptability. BJJ builds confidence, self-defense proficiency, and a supportive community, offering a holistic approach to personal growth.

Mixed Martial Arts (MMA) Classes in Union

Mixed martial arts provides a comprehensive skill set, combining striking and grappling techniques. It enhances physical fitness, mental toughness, and adaptability. MMA fosters self-confidence, self-discipline, and effective self-defense.

Muay Thai Classes in Union

Muay Thai develops exceptional striking skills, physical conditioning, and mental toughness. Practitioners gain agility, self-confidence, and effective self-defense techniques. Muay Thai offers a holistic approach to fitness and a strong sense of well-being in a supportive community.
